Facilities

🏥 24/7 Emergency and Trauma Care: Dedicated emergency unit with on-site trauma specialists, fully equipped to handle urgent medical situations and critical care.
❤️ Cardiac Care Unit: Specialized cardiac center providing comprehensive services, including diagnostics, surgery, rehabilitation, and preventive care.
🤖 Robotic Surgery Suite: Advanced minimally invasive robotic-assisted surgery options for various procedures, ensuring precision and faster recovery times.
🩺 Multi-Specialty Intensive Care Unit (ICU): State-of-the-art ICU facilities for critical care, with units for neonatal, pediatric, and adult patients.
🎗️ Oncology Department: Complete cancer care center with chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and surgical oncology services.
🦴 Orthopedic and Joint Replacement Center: Expert orthopedic care including joint replacements, spine surgeries, and sports injury treatments.
💧 Dialysis Center: Renal care services offering hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis for patients with kidney conditions.
📷 Advanced Imaging and Diagnostic Center: High-resolution MRI, CT, ultrasound, and X-ray imaging to support accurate diagnoses.
🦽 Physical Rehabilitation and Therapy: Comprehensive physical and occupational therapy services for recovery and rehabilitation post-surgery or injury.
💊 Pharmacy and Laboratory Services: In-house pharmacy and diagnostic laboratories for convenient and quick access to medications and test results.
💻 Telemedicine Services: Remote consultations and follow-ups with specialists via secure video conferencing for patient convenience.
