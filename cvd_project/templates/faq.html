📅 Appointments

How can I book an appointment? ▼
You can book an appointment in three ways:
• Online: Visit our website and click on "Book Now" on any doctor's profile
• Phone: Call us at +91 44 8765 4321
• In-person: Visit our hospital reception between 8 AM - 8 PM
Do I need a referral to see a specialist? ▼
No, you don't need a referral. You can directly book an appointment with any of our specialists. However, if you have a family doctor, we recommend keeping them informed about your cardiac care.
Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment? ▼
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any charges. Please call our appointment desk at +91 44 8765 4321 or email appointments@cardiovasc.com
What documents should I bring for my first visit? ▼
Please bring:
• Valid ID proof (Aadhar, Passport, Driver's License)
• Previous medical records (if any)
• List of current medications
• Insurance card (if applicable)

👨‍⚕️ Doctors

How do I know which doctor to choose? ▼
You can view each doctor's profile including their specialty, experience, education, and patient ratings. For complex cases, our reception team can help recommend the right specialist based on your condition.
Are the doctors at CardioVasc qualified? ▼
Yes, all our doctors are highly qualified with MBBS, MD/DM degrees from reputed institutions. Many have international training and fellowships. Their complete credentials are available on their profile pages.
Can I change my doctor after booking? ▼
Yes, you can request a change of doctor. Please contact our appointment desk at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment.

🏥 Facilities

What emergency services are available? ▼
We have 24/7 emergency services including:
• Ambulance service (call +91 44 1234 5678)
• Emergency trauma care
• Cardiac emergency unit
• 24/7 pharmacy
• ICU and critical care
Is parking available at the hospital? ▼
Yes, we have a dedicated multi-level parking facility for patients and visitors. Parking is free for all patients and attendants.
Do you have telemedicine services? ▼
Yes, we offer telemedicine consultations for follow-up appointments. You can book a video consultation through our website or by calling our appointment desk.

💰 Payments

What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept:
• Cash
• Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
• UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
• Net Banking
• Health Insurance (cashless)
Do you accept health insurance? ▼
Yes, we have tie-ups with all major insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card at the time of admission. Our insurance desk will help you with the cashless claim process.
Is there any discount for senior citizens? ▼
Yes, we offer a 10% discount on consultation fees for senior citizens (above 65 years). Please carry a valid age proof to avail the discount.

ℹ️ General

What are the hospital visiting hours? ▼
General visiting hours: 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM daily. ICU visiting hours: 5:00 PM - 5:30 PM. Only 2 visitors allowed per patient at a time.
Is the hospital wheelchair accessible? ▼
Yes, the entire hospital is wheelchair accessible with ramps, wide corridors, and accessible restrooms. Wheelchairs are available at the main entrance.
How do I provide feedback about my experience? ▼
You can provide feedback through:
• Contact Us form on our website
• Feedback box at the hospital reception
• Email: feedback@cardiovasc.com
• Call our patient relations officer: +91 44 7777 8888
