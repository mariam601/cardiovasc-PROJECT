🔒 Privacy Policy & Disclaimer

How we protect your data and use of our website

🔒 Privacy Policy

📋 Information We Collect

At CardioVasc Heart Institute, we collect the following types of information to provide better healthcare services:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, gender, contact details, address
  • Medical Information: Health records, diagnosis, treatment history, prescriptions
  • Payment Information: Billing details, payment methods, insurance information
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information (when using our website)

🛡️ How We Use Your Information

  • ✅ To provide medical diagnosis, treatment, and healthcare services
  • ✅ To schedule and manage appointments
  • ✅ To process payments and insurance claims
  • ✅ To communicate important health information and updates
  • ✅ To improve our services and patient experience
  • ✅ To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

🤝 Data Sharing & Disclosure

Your personal and medical information is kept confidential. We do not sell or rent your data to third parties. However, we may share information with:

  • 👨‍⚕️ Healthcare Providers: Doctors, nurses, and staff involved in your care
  • 🏥 Hospitals & Labs: For diagnostic tests and treatments
  • 🩺 Insurance Companies: For claim processing (with your consent)
  • ⚖️ Legal Authorities: When required by law

🔐 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • 🔒 SSL encryption for all online transactions
  • 📁 Secure servers with firewall protection
  • 👥 Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • 📋 Regular security audits and updates

🍪 Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand how you use our site. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. For pediatric care, all information is provided by parents or legal guardians.

📧 Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send you health tips, newsletters, and appointment reminders. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting us directly.

✏️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • ✅ Access your personal and medical records
  • ✅ Request correction of inaccurate information
  • ✅ Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • ✅ Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • ✅ File a complaint about data handling
